Procedure


Step

Description

Image

1

Acquire 5 petri dishes and bisect 15 planaria

2

Put 6 in each dish and label them from 0-4 hours

3

Set up 4 boxes with a LED bulb on a timer for as many hours as their group (1-4 hours)

4

Record lengths of Planaria and qualitative observations. Place 1-4 hours Planaria groups under corresponding box and the 0 hours group on table, then turn on the timer.

5

Repeat the previous step for 15 days.
